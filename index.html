Wardens of the Amber Cage

Welcome to our early preview of Wardens of the Amber Cage, a cooperative first person dungeon crawler aiming to allow both Virtual Reality and traditional screen play.

You will play the wardens of an extra-dimensional prison designed to contain the worst creatures having ever wandered in our plane of existence.

This experience was primary designed to being played with a Virtual Reality Head-Mounted Display but also support traditional screens.

Before playing, please spend a minute or two reading the Guide and why not reading the F.A.Q before you Download and try our preview build.
