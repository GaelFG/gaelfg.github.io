Gembasher - Homepage

Introduction

Gembasher is a team of wannabe video games creators. We’re currently working on our first full time project, currently named Raging Platypus Grave. Don’t ask why.

It’s a cooperative first person shooter in wich you play a team of witches and warlocks invading monsters infected undergrounds dens.

Althrougth not aiming epic scale content, it should feature tactic realtime coordination, enought varity to have somes hours of fun, was designed to be played with VR headsets and will allow you to let play your friends freely to ensure you will never have to crawl alone in this hell.
