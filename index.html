Wardens of the Amber Cage

We're happy to introduce our Virtual Reality game project 'Wardens of the Amber Cage' by releasing a demonstration version.

It's a first person cooperative action game. You play the wardens of an extra-dimensionnal prison designed to countain the worst creatures having ever wandered on our reality.

This experience was primary designed to being played with a Virtual Reality Head-Mounted Display but can also be played on standard flat screen.

Thanks for spend a minute or two reading the Guide and why not read the F.A.Q before you Download and try our demonstration.
